You can make a \cm{} quickly by following these
suggestions. First, make Charisma, Wisdom or Intelligence your

\subsection{Class features}
As a \cm, you gain the following class features.

\subsubsection{Hit Points}
\textbf{Hit Dice:} 1d6 per \cm level \\
\textbf{Hit Points at 1st Level:} 6 + your Constitution modifier \\
\textbf{Hit Points at Higher Level:} Id6 (or 4) + your
Constitution modifier per \cm level after 1st \\

the equipment granted by your background:

\end{itemize}
